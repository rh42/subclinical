# subclinical

The Institute of Questionable Neuroclarity is open for intake.

A short self-assessment from the Dept. of Vibes & Hasty Generalizations, in English and 繁體中文. You fill in Form 88-B, the Institute reviews your file, and you leave with a provisional diagnosis and an itemized receipt. The visit takes about three minutes.

No clinical validity. High emotional validity.

**[Begin intake](https://rh42.github.io/subclinical/)**

## The fine print

This is satire about being tired. It cannot diagnose you, and it will not notice if you are actually not okay. People are better at that.

Nothing you select leaves your browser. Your file is stored on your own device, which makes it legally your problem.

## Accessibility

The Institute discriminates by vibe only. Fully keyboard-operable, screen-reader labelled throughout (decorative stamps and seals are hidden, the diagnosis chart is described in words), WCAG AA contrast, and it holds still if your system asks for reduced motion. The document language follows the language toggle.
